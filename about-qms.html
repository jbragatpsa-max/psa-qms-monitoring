QMS Scope and Context

Scope Statement

The Quality Management System (QMS) of PSA Cebu Provincial Statistical Office covers the delivery of statistical services, civil registration services, and administrative support processes in compliance with ISO 9001:2015.

Interested Parties

  • Clients / Data Users
  • Employees
  • National PSA
  • Suppliers and Service Providers
  • Regulatory Bodies

Quality Policy

The PSA Cebu Provincial Statistical Office is committed to providing quality statistical and civil registration services that meet customer and regulatory requirements.
