Human Resource Management

ISO Focus: Clauses 7.2 and 7.3

Process Description

The Human Resource Management process of PSA Cebu Provincial Statistical Office ensures that personnel performing work affecting QMS performance are competent, properly trained, and aware of their roles and responsibilities.

Recruitment, Deployment, Training, and Evaluation

  • Recruitment and selection of qualified personnel
  • Deployment based on competency and assignment
  • Provision of necessary training and development
  • Performance evaluation and monitoring

Competence and Awareness

  • Competency Matrix
  • Training Plan
  • Training Attendance Records
